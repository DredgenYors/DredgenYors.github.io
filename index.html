Teaching

A course is a nature park, the students are park visitors, and the instructor is a well-trained guide. The guide's goal is to get everyone to a beautiful vista, while being mindful of the strengths and weaknesses of the visitors. Some of the visitors may want to climb steep cliffs, while others may just want to walk the well-worn paths. Similarly, educators instruct students, connecting them to their goals while navigating the barriers and the strengths/weaknesses of the students along the way. Accordingly, my teaching and mentoring goal is to create a learning environment that is driven by the needs of the students.
